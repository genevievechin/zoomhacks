Trouble Shoot

Joining Meetings

1) After opening the Zoom application on your desktop, you should see 4 boxes labeled ‘New Meeting’, ‘Join’, ‘Schedule’, and ‘Share Screen’. Click on the top-right box labeled ‘Join’.

2) Now two text boxes will appear to you. The first one is where you will type in your Meeting ID, this is the unique numeric code that your Zoom meeting room is assigned to. (If you do not know what your meeting code is, ask your teacher again! They may have forgotten to provide it.)

3) Alternatively, you can press the small down arrow next to this textbox to view and select a meeting room from your recent Zoom call history, without having to type in the meeting code again. On the textbox below this, you can type in or edit your display name. Make sure you use your real name, so your teacher knows who you are and can let you in the meeting room!

4) There are two checkboxes below, this is where you can choose not to connect to audio, or to turn off your video. But don’t worry, you can change these settings again once you’re in the call too. Press the blue ‘Join’ button and wait to be let in!
